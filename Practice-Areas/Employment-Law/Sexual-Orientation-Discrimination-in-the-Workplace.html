Sexual Orientation Discrimination in the Workplace

Individuals who identify with the LGBT community are protected from harassment in the workplace by Minnesota state law. If you are lesbian, gay, bisexual or transgender and experiencing sexual orientation discrimination at work, our lawyers can talk to you about your rights and options.

LGBT employees throughout Minnesota can turn to the Twin Cities-based firm of Gregg M. Corwin & Associate Law Office, P.C., for help asserting their rights. Our Minneapolis and St. Paul workplace LGBT rights attorneys can inform you of your rights and options under the law. Contact us online to schedule a consultation.

You Are Protected Under Minnesota State Law

People who are lesbian, gay, bisexual or transgender who have been victims of discrimination and/or harassment at work are protected by state law. If you have experienced any of the following and you believe it is due to your sexual orientation, please talk to our attorneys to explore your options:

  • Sexual harassment
  • Discrimination
  • Wrongful termination
  • Retaliation
  • Failure to promote
  • Failure to hire

Transgender Discrimination

Are you transitioning at work? Whether you are a woman who your co-workers previously knew as a man, or whether you are a man who your co-workers previously knew as a woman, you are protected from discrimination in the workplace. Whether you are being harassed about the bathroom you are using, the clothes you are wearing or the choices you have made, if you are having problems at work, please talk to us to explore your rights and options.
