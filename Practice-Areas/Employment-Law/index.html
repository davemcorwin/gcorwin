Employment Law

Making your voice heard as an employee in a company, no matter what size, can seem like a daunting task. But when you are trying to speak out against unethical behavior, illegal actions or violations to the basic rights of employees regardless of gender, race or national origin, being heard is critical.

At the firm of Gregg M. Corwin & Associate Law Office, P.C., in Minneapolis, Minnesota, we have been giving a voice to wronged employees since 1972. We want to help give you the leverage you need to be heard and ensure that unlawful behavior in the workplace is eliminated. Contact our Minnesota employment law attorneys today for information on how we may serve you.

Public and Private Employment Law Matters

Our firm represents clients in the private and public sectors in a variety of legal matters. Relentlessly pursuing favorable verdicts and settlements for our clients, we are aggressive and focused. We have represented countless employees in Minnesota in matters such as:

  • Wrongful discharge
  • Hostile work environment

  • Federal employee matters
  • Severance agreements

  • Employment contracts and noncompete agreements

  • Airline Employees and the Railway Labor Act
  • Education law
