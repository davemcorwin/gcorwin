Firm Overview

Minneapolis Labor and Employment Lawyers

Since 1972, labor and employment law attorney Gregg M. Corwin has been assisting employees and union members throughout Minnesota. Our firm, located in St. Louis Park, Minnesota, is widely considered one of the state's leading employment law firms.

Our experience, tenacity, dedication and objectivity have been used to the advantage of numerous clients for over 40 years. If you would like more information regarding our legal services, please contact us today. Call 952-544-7774 .

What You Can Expect From Our Firm

Our clients come to us at a very stressful time in their lives. Many have either been separated from the employment or are facing a professional licensing action that may jeopardize their employment. We are very sensitive to the challenges our clients are facing. When our clients meet with us, they know they are getting more than an individual lawyer working on their behalf. They get an entire team.

We believe a team approach yields the best results for our clients as we are able to explore a legal issue from every angle. This also enables us to be efficient, providing a solid legal road map when time is of the essence.

A Twin Cities Employment Law Firm That is Available

Our firm is available to help clients by providing information regarding labor and employment law, apprising clients of their options for the resolution of their legal issues (such as sexual harassment or wage and hour claims) and providing support, whether their case must be handled in court or through arbitration and mediation. Our services to employees address the following issues:

  • Employment agreements
  • Discrimination
  • Employee privacy
  • Professional licensing
  • Airline employees
  • Education law

A Tough Litigator — A Diplomatic Mediator

Employment and labor law attorney Gregg Corwin is dedicated to aggressively representing employees, unions and union members with matters requiring litigation. But this only describes one facet of his character. As a mediator and arbitrator, Gregg Corwin is able to examine matters from a variety of angles and help clients find resolution before situations erupt and can only be handled in court.
